Vegan Mac n Cheese

Creamy and delicious vegan mac n cheese is the ultimate comfort food!

The vegan version of this recipe contains up to 40% less calories compared to a regular mac n cheese.

You can use any pasta shape you like!

Ingredients:

  • 500g any pasta shape (use lentil pasta for a protein boost!)
  • 2 large starchy potatoes
  • 1 large carrot
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 5 Tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 3 Tbsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation:

  1. Roughly cut the potatoes and the carrot into even pieces so they boil evenly.
  2. Boil them in salted water until fork-tender and drain.
  3. In a blender add the potatoes and the carrot along with the oil.
  4. Blend the mixture until smooth.
  5. Add the spices and nutritional yeast.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. If the sauce is too thick add some water, if it is too runny, you can add some potato or corn starch.
  8. Boil your pasta according to package instructions.
  9. Mix the pasta with the sauce.
  10. Plate and enjoy!
